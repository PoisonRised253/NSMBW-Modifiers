##Settings Stuff##
0xX00 Section:
X=0: Default
X=1: Block_Hit Upwards entry animation
X=3: Propeller Straight upwards spawn
X=4: Star & Propeller / Items Drops straight down
X=5: All Items fall fast, straight down
X=6&7: Propeller doesnt float/abides by the laws of physics, doesnt move, but colides with ground (67 not mentioned on purpose, i hate that meme)
X=8&9: Block_Hit "Unboxing"

0x0000X000 Section:
X=F: Item disappears upon touching ground // Maybe

0x000000X0 Section:
X=0: Default
X=6: No X Velocity // Just a game theory
X=7: No X Vel, Slow Fall // Also Just a game theory

0x0X000000 Section:
X=1: Right-ward yeetus
X=2: Freeze / No-Collide (Basically just static, png-like powerups)
X=3: Upwards-Right yeetus
X=4: Left-ward yeetus
X=5: Short Up-right yeet
X=6: strong down-right yeet
X=7: Left yeetus, but they come back... ish
X=8: Drop down chaotically
X=9: Block_Hit Unboxing???
X=F: Spawn NOTHING

0xX0000000: No Effect
